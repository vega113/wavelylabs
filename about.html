About WavelyLabs

A software studio focused on applied AI.

WavelyLabs is an independent software studio focused on applied artificial intelligence. We build AI-powered products designed to improve communication, content workflows, and digital interaction.

Current Products

SlidesLab

AI-powered slide generation for structured presentations. The product supports multi-variant slide generation, review workflows, and export-ready output including PDF/ZIP flows with transparent credit-based usage.

slideslab.ai

Tube2Web

Turn YouTube channels and playlists into AI-powered websites. The product includes ingestion/import workflows, creator dashboards, transcript and article generation, semantic search, and AI chat experiences for viewers.

tube2web.ai
