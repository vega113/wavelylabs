Operator and Data Controller

WavelyLabs is operated by Yuri Zelikov (sole proprietor), based in Tel Aviv, Israel. For privacy or data requests, contact hello@wavelylabs.com.

Data We Collect

  • Contact and correspondence data (emails sent to support or legal contacts)
  • Website analytics and security logs (IP address, browser/device metadata, request logs)
  • Operational data needed to maintain services and prevent abuse

Product-specific account and usage data is governed by each product's own policy on SlidesLab and Tube2Web.

How We Use Data

  • Operate and secure the website and related services
  • Respond to support, legal, and compliance requests
  • Monitor performance and diagnose reliability issues
  • Comply with legal obligations and fraud-prevention requirements

Third-Party Processors

Depending on the service path, WavelyLabs uses infrastructure and processing providers such as Cloudflare, Supabase, OpenAI, Google, and payment Merchant of Record providers (Lemon Squeezy or Polar). Each provider processes data under its own contractual terms and privacy policy.

Cookies and Analytics

We use essential technical storage for security and site operation. Limited analytics may be used to understand reliability and usage patterns. You can control non-essential cookies through browser settings where applicable.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for service operation, support handling, legal obligations, and fraud prevention. Data that is no longer needed is deleted or anonymized on a rolling basis.

Security

We use standard administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards, including encrypted transport (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and operational logging. No system is 100% secure.

International Transfers

Infrastructure providers may process data in multiple jurisdictions. By using the services, you acknowledge that data may be transferred and processed outside your country.

Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, export, or restriction of personal data. Send requests to hello@wavelylabs.com.

Children's Privacy

WavelyLabs services are not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us so we can investigate and remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material updates are reflected by the "Last updated" date on this page.
